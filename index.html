Support & Documentation

▸ How does OCR Verification work?

Cross-references ledger amounts against scanned PDF receipts using Vision Transformers (ViT) for pixel-level manipulation detection.

▸ What are Graph Neural Networks (GNNs)?

FraudGNN-RL analyzes entire payment networks as graphs. If Employee A → Vendor B → Employee C, the GNN flags the shell-company ring. RL dynamically adjusts thresholds.

▸ How does Federated Learning protect data?

FinGraphFL trains models across bank nodes without sharing raw data. Only encrypted gradients are exchanged via Secure FedAvg with Rényi DP (ε=0.5).

▸ What is Fully Homomorphic Encryption?

FHE (CKKS lattice) allows AI to compute on encrypted data. Your ledger is never decrypted during analysis — the gold standard of Data-in-Use protection.

▸ XAI Audit Trail & EU AI Act compliance?

SHAP/LIME feature attributions on every flagged transaction. Satisfies EU AI Act 2025 & GDPR Article 22 right to explanation. Full audit-by-design architecture.
